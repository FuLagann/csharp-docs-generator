    A base constructor for creating a quaternion using euler angles (yaw, pitch, and roll).

    public Quaternion(float yaw, float pitch, float roll);

    Parameters

    float yaw

    Rotation in radians around the y axis (up and down). Yaw rotation is like a boat rotating around on a flat plane sea.

    float pitch

    Rotation in radians around the x axis (left and right). Pitch rotation is like a pitcher of water motioning over to pour water.

    float roll

    Rotation in radians around the z axis (forward and backward). Roll rotation is like a plane doing rolls (spinning around while staying in trajectory).
